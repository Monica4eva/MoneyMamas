Why This Workshop Matters

Too many moms feel stuck on paycheck‐to‐paycheck, juggling diapers and bills but never quite making ends meet. That financial stress bleeds into every corner—family outings get canceled, dreams get sidelined, and peace of mind becomes a luxury.

Pain Points We Tackle

  • Never-ending expenses: Groceries, utilities, school fees,business costs … it never stops.
  • No clear plan: Money feels like a mystery, not a tool.
  • Zero savings: Emergencies hit hard when you have no buffer.

How You’ll Win

  • Mindset shift: Unpack limiting beliefs and build abundance habits.
  • Hands-on budgeting: From envelopes to apps—you’ll find what sticks.
  • Actionable goals: Real milestones you can celebrate, Pula by Pula.

By the end of seven weeks, you’ll have a personalized money roadmap, a community cheering you on, and—a renewed financial confidence to pass on to your kids.
