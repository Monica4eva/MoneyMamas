Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains:

1. What We Collect

  • Personal Info: Name, email address, phone number when you register or sign up.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, and interaction events (via cookies).

2. How We Use It

  • To deliver the workshop, send updates, and personalize your experience.
  • To analyze site performance and improve our content.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies to keep you logged in and to track anonymous analytics (via Google Analytics). You can opt out via your browser settings.

4. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. We may share with:

  • Our trusted email delivery service to send you course updates.
  • Law enforcement if required by law.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you.
  • Ask for corrections or deletion.
  • Opt-out of marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link.
